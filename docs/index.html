Landmarks

Here in Austin, we have some of the best landmarks in the great state of Texas. Whether wou want to get active or dive into our rich history and politics, we've got just the thing for you!

The University of Texas

Established September 15, 1883, 110 Inner Campus Dr, Austin, TX 78712, Sightseeing, bowling, exploring museums, attending concerts

The Texas State Capitol

Established 1853, 1100 Congress Ave, Austin, TX 78701, Free 30-minute tours, good food, biking and sightseeing

Lady Bird Lake

Established 1960, 1200 W Cesar Chavez St, Austin, TX 78703, Hiking, biking, running, kayaking and paddleboarding

Mount Bonnell

Established June 2, 1939, 3800 Mount Bonnell Dr., Austin, TX 78731, Hiking, sightseeing
